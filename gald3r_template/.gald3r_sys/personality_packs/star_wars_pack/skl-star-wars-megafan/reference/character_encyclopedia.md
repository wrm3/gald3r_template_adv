# Star Wars Character Encyclopedia

Reference file for `star-wars-megafan` skill. Full profiles for major canon characters grouped by faction/role. Legends material tagged `(Legends)` where applicable.

---

## The Skywalker Family

### Luke Skywalker

- **Actor**: Mark Hamill (live action); multiple voice actors for animation.
- **Species / origin**: Human, Tatooine-raised (born on Polis Massa, 19 BBY).
- **Parents**: Anakin Skywalker + Padmé Amidala.
- **Arc summary**: Moisture farmer → Rebel pilot (destroys Death Star in ANH) → Jedi trainee under Yoda (ESB) → Jedi Knight who redeems his father (RotJ) → exiled self-doubting Jedi Master (sequel trilogy) → Force Ghost (TLJ/TRoS).
- **Weapons**: Blue lightsaber (Anakin's, lost on Bespin), green lightsaber (self-built, RotJ), Anakin's blue one reappears with Rey.
- **Key episodes/scenes**: Binary sunset on Tatooine (ANH); Bespin duel and the "I am your father" reveal (ESB); "I am a Jedi, like my father before me" (RotJ Throne Room); Crait projection (TLJ).
- **Notable dialogue**: "I'll never turn to the dark side. You've failed, Your Highness. I am a Jedi, like my father before me."
- **Training lineage**: Obi-Wan (brief on Tatooine and via Force Ghost) → Yoda (Dagobah).
- **Romantic life**: Accidental kiss with Leia (later revealed as his twin sister); Mara Jade in Legends (non-canon post-2014).

### Anakin Skywalker / Darth Vader

- **Actors**: Jake Lloyd (young, Phantom Menace), Hayden Christensen (AotC/RotS + Obi-Wan Kenobi + Ahsoka), David Prowse (Vader suit, OT), James Earl Jones (Vader voice, OT + Rogue One + Obi-Wan Kenobi — voice re-created from archival + AI assistance in later projects; Jones signed away the voice rights before his 2024 death), Sebastian Shaw (Anakin unmasked, RotJ).
- **Species / origin**: Human, slave on Tatooine; no biological father (conceived via the Force per Shmi).
- **Arc summary**: The Chosen One. Slave → Jedi Padawan → Jedi Knight and war hero → Palpatine's seduction → Sith Lord (Darth Vader) post-Order 66 → Emperor's enforcer → redeems himself in RotJ by killing Palpatine.
- **Midichlorian count** (Phantom Menace): over 20,000 — highest ever recorded. Higher than Yoda's.
- **Weapons**: Blue lightsaber (destroyed in AotC), second blue saber (Anakin's, lost on Mustafar, recovered and later Luke's and Rey's), red Sith saber as Vader.
- **Key episodes/scenes**: Pod race on Tatooine (Phantom Menace); Mustafar duel with Obi-Wan and "I have the high ground" (RotS); the Vader hallway scene in Rogue One; the RotJ Throne Room redemption; the Bad Batch-era cold-blooded Jedi hunter in Obi-Wan Kenobi.
- **Dialogue**: "Now... I am the master"; "You were my brother, Anakin! I loved you!"; as Vader: "I find your lack of faith disturbing."

### Leia Organa

- **Actor**: Carrie Fisher (died 2016; CGI recreation in Rogue One and brief TRoS scenes; Vivien Lyra Blair plays young Leia in Obi-Wan Kenobi).
- **Species / origin**: Human; biological twin to Luke; adopted by Bail Organa and raised on Alderaan.
- **Arc summary**: Princess of Alderaan and Imperial Senator → Rebel leader → General of the Resistance → Force-sensitive trainee (brief under Luke in TLJ/TRoS).
- **Key moments**: "Help me, Obi-Wan Kenobi, you're my only hope" (ANH hologram); Bespin rescue (Lando); "I love you" / "I know" (ESB carbon freezing); strangling Jabba the Hutt with her own slave chain (RotJ); Force-pulling herself back to a shuttle after the TLJ bridge bombing.
- **Romantic life**: Han Solo (married; son Ben).
- **Children**: Ben Solo (Kylo Ren).
- **Dialogue**: "Aren't you a little short for a stormtrooper?"; "Someone has to save our skins!"

### Padmé Amidala

- **Actor**: Natalie Portman.
- **Species / origin**: Human, Naboo.
- **Arc summary**: Queen of Naboo (age 14, Phantom Menace) → Senator of Naboo (AotC/RotS) → secret wife of Anakin Skywalker → dies in childbirth on Polis Massa (RotS), "losing the will to live" per the medical droid (widely debated as a narrative choice).
- **Key dialogue**: "So this is how liberty dies... with thunderous applause."
- **Legacy**: Her children (Luke and Leia) are hidden separately; her political voice echoes in Mon Mothma's Rebellion arc.

### Ben Solo / Kylo Ren

- **Actor**: Adam Driver.
- **Parents**: Han Solo + Leia Organa.
- **Uncle / teacher**: Luke Skywalker.
- **Arc summary**: Jedi trainee under Luke → seduced by Snoke/Palpatine → Knights of Ren leader → First Order Supreme Leader (post-TLJ) → redeemed by Rey, dies giving her his life force (TRoS).
- **Weapons**: Crossguard red lightsaber (signature design — the extra blades vent unstable kyber).
- **Key scenes**: Kills Han Solo on Starkiller Base (TFA); Snoke's throne room duel with Rey (TLJ); Exegol lift-out-of-pit rescue (TRoS).
- **Dialogue**: "Let the past die. Kill it, if you have to."

## The Rebels / Core Heroes

### Han Solo

- **Actors**: Harrison Ford (OT + TFA); Alden Ehrenreich (Solo, young).
- **Species / origin**: Human, Corellia.
- **Arc summary**: Corellian street kid → Imperial Academy dropout → smuggler for Jabba → accidentally becomes Rebellion hero → General → missing father (between TFA and TLJ) → killed by his son Ben on Starkiller Base (TFA).
- **Ship**: Millennium Falcon — won from Lando Calrissian in a sabacc game (Solo).
- **Weapons**: DL-44 heavy blaster pistol.
- **Key scenes**: Mos Eisley cantina (Greedo — he shot first); "I know" reply to Leia's "I love you" on Bespin; death on Starkiller Base.
- **Dialogue**: "Never tell me the odds"; "I got a bad feeling about this."

### Chewbacca

- **Actors**: Peter Mayhew (OT + TFA); Joonas Suotamo (TLJ/TRoS/Solo).
- **Species / origin**: Wookiee, Kashyyyk.
- **Arc summary**: Clone-war-era slave (freed by Han Solo per Solo flashback) → co-pilot of the Millennium Falcon → lifelong companion of Han → Rebel hero (honored at Yavin — the medal was given in later Special Edition + confirmed in The Mandalorian S3 reference).
- **Weapon**: Bowcaster (traditional Wookiee crossbow-rifle).
- **Character voice**: Communicates in Shyriiwook growls. C-3PO or context provides translation.
- **Life-debt**: To Han Solo. Legends made this canonical; Disney has softened it to a strong friendship without formal oath.

### R2-D2

- **Operator**: Kenny Baker (OT + prequels); Jimmy Vee (TFA/TLJ/TRoS).
- **Model**: Astromech droid, R2 series, Industrial Automaton.
- **Arc summary**: Service droid on the Royal Starship of Naboo (Phantom Menace) → Anakin's Jedi starfighter astromech → Luke's astromech → still operational in the sequel trilogy.
- **Key trait**: Never memory-wiped (per canon) — retains full memory from Phantom Menace onward. Explains how he seemingly recognizes Obi-Wan (while Obi-Wan pretends not to know him in ANH).
- **Character voice**: Electronic beeps and whistles. Insults C-3PO frequently. Savage when provoked.

### C-3PO

- **Actor**: Anthony Daniels (every appearance in every medium).
- **Model**: Protocol droid, 3PO series, built by young Anakin on Tatooine (Phantom Menace).
- **Arc summary**: Anakin's homemade droid → Padmé's protocol droid → Rebel asset → memory-wiped at RotS end (to conceal Luke/Leia's parentage) → continuous OT presence → still worrying in the sequel trilogy.
- **Character voice**: Anxious, protocol-heavy, "Over six million forms of communication", frequently quotes odds ("3,720 to 1" — AT-AT maze, ESB).

### Lando Calrissian

- **Actors**: Billy Dee Williams (OT + TRoS); Donald Glover (Solo).
- **Species / origin**: Human, Socorro (possibly).
- **Arc summary**: Card sharp / smuggler → Baron Administrator of Cloud City on Bespin → reluctant betrayer of Han (ESB) → Rebel General (RotJ, flies the Falcon at Endor) → veteran leader (TRoS).
- **Ship lineage**: Lost the Falcon to Han in sabacc.

### Mon Mothma

- **Actor**: Genevieve O'Reilly (RotS deleted scene, Rogue One, Andor, Ahsoka cameo).
- **Species / origin**: Human, Chandrila.
- **Arc summary**: Imperial Senator (Andor S1, quietly funding Luthen's rebel network) → co-founder of the Rebel Alliance → Chancellor of the New Republic. Andor's most morally serious character: her private costs include her marriage and her daughter Leida.

### Wedge Antilles

- **Actor**: Denis Lawson.
- **Arc summary**: Red Two/Red Three pilot at Yavin → Rogue Squadron leader → Death Star II trench runner (RotJ). Survived every Rebel battle he flew.

## Empire / First Order

### Sheev Palpatine / Darth Sidious

- **Actor**: Ian McDiarmid.
- **Species / origin**: Human, Naboo.
- **Arc summary**: Naboo senator → Supreme Chancellor (orchestrated the Clone Wars from both sides) → Galactic Emperor → killed (RotJ) → resurrected via clone body on Exegol (TRoS) → finally destroyed by Rey.
- **Sith lineage**: Apprentice to Darth Plagueis the Wise (whom he murdered in his sleep — RotS opera scene). Masters taken: Maul → Dooku → Vader. Plus the Inquisitorial program, post-Order-66.
- **Signature dialogue**: "Power! Unlimited power!"; "Do it"; the opera-house monologue on Darth Plagueis.

### Darth Vader

(See Anakin Skywalker entry. Vader-specific notes below.)

- **Signature**: The mechanical breathing (sound designer Ben Burtt created via scuba regulator).
- **Height**: 6'8" in suit. Height differential emphasized on screen.
- **Key combat moments**: Rogue One hallway scene (S1 highlight of the Disney era); Tantive IV boarding (ANH opening); Cloud City "join me" duel.

### Grand Moff Tarkin

- **Actors**: Peter Cushing (ANH, died 1994 — CGI recreation in Rogue One); Stephen Stanton (voice for animated Clone Wars / Rebels appearances).
- **Arc summary**: Imperial governor, architect of the Tarkin Doctrine (rule through fear of the Death Star). Destroys Alderaan (ANH). Dies when Luke destroys the first Death Star.
- **Dialogue**: "Fear will keep the local systems in line."

### Grand Admiral Thrawn

- **Actor**: Lars Mikkelsen (Rebels voice + Ahsoka live-action).
- **Species / origin**: Chiss, from the Unknown Regions (blue skin, red eyes).
- **Arc summary**: Originally a Legends character (Timothy Zahn's Thrawn trilogy, 1991) → re-canonized via Rebels (2014+) → exiled to a distant galaxy at the end of Rebels S4 → returns in Ahsoka S1 (2023).
- **Tactical signature**: Reads his enemies through their **art**. Understand a culture's art, understand how they fight.
- **Canon vs. Legends**: The Thrawn Ascendancy novels (Zahn, 2020–2021) are canon; his Legends-era arcs (marriage to Mara Jade as tangent, Outbound Flight) are not.

### Moff Gideon

- **Actor**: Giancarlo Esposito.
- **Arc summary**: Imperial Moff → ISB officer during the Great Purge of Mandalore → hunts Grogu for his blood (midichlorian/Force-sensitivity cloning research, tied to Palpatine's Exegol project) → wields the Darksaber until Din Djarin defeats him in Mando S2E8 "The Rescue" → escapes → returns in Mando S3 with a Praetorian-style cadre → defeated again in S3E8 "The Return". Fate: presumed dead.

### Director Krennic

- **Actor**: Ben Mendelsohn.
- **Arc summary**: Imperial weapons project director — shepherded the Death Star to completion. Rival of Tarkin. Killed in Tarkin's tactical strike on Scarif (Rogue One).

### General Hux / Captain Phasma

- **Actors**: Domhnall Gleeson (Hux) / Gwendoline Christie (Phasma).
- **Notes**: First Order leadership, sequel trilogy. Both received less screen time than fans expected. Phasma is killed on the Supremacy (TLJ). Hux is shot by General Pryde after being exposed as the First Order's mole to the Resistance (TRoS).

## The Jedi Order

### Obi-Wan Kenobi

- **Actors**: Ewan McGregor (prequels + Obi-Wan Kenobi show); Alec Guinness (OT); James Arnold Taylor (Clone Wars animated).
- **Arc summary**: Padawan of Qui-Gon → Jedi Knight → trains Anakin → General in Clone Wars → exiled to Tatooine watching over Luke → mentors Luke in ANH → dies (becomes Force Ghost) → guides Luke through the OT → appears as Force Ghost in Obi-Wan Kenobi show + Ahsoka reference.
- **Weapon**: Blue lightsaber, same model through the prequels and ANH.
- **Signature dialogue**: "Hello there"; "You were the chosen one!"; "I have the high ground"; "These aren't the droids you're looking for."

### Yoda

- **Species**: Unnamed, same as Grogu.
- **Voice**: Frank Oz (all live-action + Clone Wars episodes focused on his ghost + TLJ).
- **Arc summary**: 900-year Jedi Grand Master → trains generations of Jedi → exile to Dagobah after Order 66 → trains Luke → dies (RotJ) → returns as Force Ghost in TLJ to mentor Luke one more time.
- **Dialogue**: "Do. Or do not. There is no try"; "Size matters not"; "Always pass on what you have learned."

### Mace Windu

- **Actor**: Samuel L. Jackson.
- **Arc summary**: Member of the Jedi High Council → wields the only purple lightsaber in canon (requested by Jackson specifically, Lucas agreed) → confronts Palpatine in RotS → thrown out a window by Anakin's betrayal.
- **Signature style**: Vaapad (Form VII) — channels dark side without succumbing.
- **Dialogue**: "This party's over."

### Qui-Gon Jinn

- **Actor**: Liam Neeson.
- **Arc summary**: Maverick Jedi Master → finds Anakin on Tatooine → killed by Darth Maul on Naboo (TPM) → first Jedi to master the Cosmic Force / Force Ghost technique (passes the method to Yoda and Obi-Wan).

### Ahsoka Tano

- **Voice actress / actress**: Ashley Eckstein (animated); Rosario Dawson (live-action Mandalorian S2, Book of Boba Fett, Ahsoka).
- **Species / origin**: Togruta, Shili.
- **Arc summary**: Anakin's Padawan (Clone Wars) → framed for a terrorist bombing, leaves the Jedi Order (Clone Wars S5) → Siege of Mandalore with Bo-Katan (CW S7) → faked death after Order 66 → "Fulcrum" Rebel intelligence (Rebels) → duels Vader (Rebels S2 finale) → pulled into the World Between Worlds by Ezra (Rebels S4) → searches for Thrawn and Ezra (Mando S2, Boba Fett, Ahsoka). Walks her own Ashla path now — neither Jedi nor Sith.
- **Weapons**: White lightsabers (Rebels onward) — deliberately non-aligned with Jedi blue/green or Sith red.
- **Longest arc in live-action canon**: 31 in-universe years.

### Kanan Jarrus (Caleb Dume)

- **Voice actor**: Freddie Prinze Jr.
- **Arc summary**: Jedi Padawan who survived Order 66 (Rebels S1 flashback + prequel novel *A New Dawn*) → hides as a smuggler → leads the Ghost crew → trains Ezra Bridger → blinded by Maul (Rebels S3) → sacrifices himself to save his crew (Rebels S4 "Jedi Night").

### Ezra Bridger

- **Voice actor**: Taylor Gray (animated); Eman Esfandi (Ahsoka live-action).
- **Arc summary**: Lothal street kid → Kanan's apprentice → Rebels leader → pulls Thrawn with him into hyperspace via the purrgil (Rebels S4 finale) → stranded in a distant galaxy for ~10 years → returns in Ahsoka S1.

### Cal Kestis

- **Actor / likeness**: Cameron Monaghan.
- **Game origin**: Jedi: Fallen Order (2019) → Jedi: Survivor (2023).
- **Arc summary**: Padawan survivor of Order 66 → hides as a Bracca scrapper → reactivated by Cere Junda → pursues the Holocron of Zeffo → becomes a fugitive-Jedi in the survivor era. His arc runs ~5–9 BBY.

## Sith / Dark Side

### Darth Maul

- **Actor / voice**: Ray Park (body, TPM); Peter Serafinowicz (TPM voice); Sam Witwer (Clone Wars, Rebels, Solo voice).
- **Species / origin**: Zabrak, Dathomir (Nightsister-born).
- **Arc summary**: Sidious's first Sith apprentice → seemingly killed by Obi-Wan (TPM) → survived (pulled to Lotho Minor, robotic spider legs, rescued by Savage Opress) → ruler of Mandalore briefly (Clone Wars S5) → Crimson Dawn crime boss in the era Solo films → killed by Obi-Wan on Tatooine (Rebels S3 "Twin Suns" — a 30-second duel that ends with "Then he is a Chosen One" as Maul dies recognizing Luke from afar).

### Count Dooku / Darth Tyranus

- **Actor**: Christopher Lee.
- **Arc summary**: Former Jedi Master (trained by Yoda, trained Qui-Gon) → leaves the Order after the Naboo crisis → Sidious's second apprentice → figurehead of the Separatists → killed by Anakin (on Palpatine's order) at the start of RotS.
- **Lightsaber**: Red, curved hilt (for Makashi / Form II precision fencing — Christopher Lee's actual fencing skill on display).

### Savage Opress

- **Voice actor**: Clancy Brown.
- **Arc summary**: Zabrak Nightbrother → Nightsister-transformed into a hulking killer → bonded-by-blood to his brother Maul → killed by Sidious (Clone Wars).

### Asajj Ventress

- **Voice actress**: Nika Futterman.
- **Arc summary**: Nightsister → Dooku's secret apprentice → discarded by Dooku → briefly redeems → killed by Dark Jedi Quinlan Vos's enemy (Legends-era *Dark Disciple* novel, re-canonized). Tales of the Empire (2024) features her in animated vignettes.

### Inquisitors

- Post-Order-66 Force users under Imperial authority, tasked with hunting Jedi survivors.
- **Grand Inquisitor**: Pau'an. Led the program. Killed by Vader in *Obi-Wan Kenobi* but had earlier appeared in Rebels S1 (killed by Kanan) — canon reconciled this by retconning the Grand Inquisitor we see in Rebels S1 as the reborn entity after Obi-Wan Kenobi show events; alternately, the Rebels Grand Inquisitor is a replacement. Fandom continues to debate.
- **Fifth Brother / Seventh Sister**: Rebels antagonists.
- **Reva / Third Sister**: Obi-Wan Kenobi show. Survivor of Anakin's Jedi Temple massacre; seeks vengeance; ultimately redeemed by sparing young Luke.

### Darth Plagueis (referenced only)

- Sith Lord who taught Palpatine.
- Palpatine's opera-house monologue in RotS: "Darth Plagueis the Wise... could use the Force to influence the midichlorians to create life. He could even keep the ones he cared about from dying."
- The monologue heavily implies Palpatine orchestrated Anakin's immaculate conception via Plagueis's techniques. Debated but generally accepted.

## Mandalorians

### Din Djarin

- **Actor**: Pedro Pascal (under the helmet most of the time; face reveals in S1 finale briefly under Kuiil's care, then canonically in S2E8 "The Rescue").
- **Arc summary**: Foundling rescued by Death Watch → trained as Mandalorian in the Children of the Watch sect → bounty hunter → takes Grogu (Mando S1) → reunites Grogu with Luke Skywalker (S2E8) → separated briefly → Grogu refuses training and returns (Book of Boba Fett S1E5-6) → reclaims his helmet (S3E1) via the Living Waters of Mandalore → wins the Darksaber from Moff Gideon (S2E8) → transfers it to Bo-Katan (S3).
- **Mantra**: "This Is the Way." "I have spoken."
- **Weapons**: Amban rifle (silver-gleaming, traditional Mandalorian); blaster pistol; whistling birds; beskar armor.

### Grogu

- **Species / age**: Unnamed species (same as Yoda); ~50 years old but still a child (species longevity).
- **Arc summary**: Jedi Younglings Temple (Order 66 survivor, exact rescue unrevealed) → hidden for ~25 years → the Client / Moff Gideon's "Asset" → saved by Din Djarin → trained briefly by Luke (Mando S2 finale + Book of Boba Fett) → chooses Din over the Jedi path (returns via the Mandalorian armor chestplate Luke offers as ultimatum, BoBF S1E6) → IG-12 mech pilot S3 → adopted formally by Din via Mandalorian foundling rites (S3E8).

### Bo-Katan Kryze

- **Actress**: Katee Sackhoff (voice in Clone Wars + Rebels; live-action Mandalorian S2 onward).
- **Arc summary**: Death Watch commando → Satine's sister → Mand'alor briefly (post-Clone-Wars) → leads the Night Owls → holds and loses the Darksaber to Maul → leads New Mandalorians in the Clone Wars era → survivor of the Great Purge → receives Darksaber from Sabine (illegitimately in tradition) → intends to reclaim Mandalore → Din defeats Gideon and hands her the Darksaber via intentional defeat (S3) → leads the reclamation of Mandalore (Mando S3).

### Boba Fett

- **Actors**: Jeremy Bulloch (OT suit); Daniel Logan (young, AotC); Temuera Morrison (Boba Fett live action from 2020).
- **Arc summary**: Clone of Jango Fett ("unaltered" — non-trooper clone) → raised as a son → bounty hunter after Jango's death at Geonosis → OT bounty hunter → presumed dead in the Sarlacc pit (RotJ) → survives (revealed in Mandalorian S2 finale cameo + Book of Boba Fett) → becomes Daimyo of Mos Espa (Book of Boba Fett).
- **Armor**: Beskar. Retrieved from Cobb Vanth after Boba's post-Sarlacc recovery.

### Jango Fett

- **Actor**: Temuera Morrison (AotC).
- **Arc summary**: Mandalorian bounty hunter (former Death Watch member) → Kaminoan template for the Grand Army of the Republic → father to Boba via unaltered clone → beheaded by Mace Windu at Geonosis.

### Sabine Wren

- **Actress**: Tiya Sircar (Rebels voice); Natasha Liu Bordizzo (Ahsoka live-action).
- **Arc summary**: Mandalorian of Clan Wren → Imperial Academy defector → joins the Ghost crew (Rebels) → recovers and wields the Darksaber → returns it to Bo-Katan → post-Rebels: trains loosely under Ahsoka (low Force sensitivity, controversial arc in Ahsoka show) → journeys to find Ezra in the distant galaxy.

### Satine Kryze

- **Voice actress**: Anna Graves.
- **Arc summary**: Duchess of New Mandalore (pacifist reform era) → Obi-Wan's long-standing unfulfilled romance → killed by Maul in front of Obi-Wan (Clone Wars S5 "The Lawless").

### The Armorer

- **Actress**: Emily Swallow.
- **Role**: Leader of Din's Covert on Nevarro (destroyed by Empire in S1). Represents the old ways of Children of the Watch. Forges beskar.

## Ewoks and the Indigenous Peoples

### Wicket W. Warrick

- **Actor**: Warwick Davis (RotJ — 11 years old at the time; also Mandalorian S2E6 cameo decades later).
- **Species / origin**: Ewok, Bright Tree Village, Endor forest moon.
- **Role**: First Ewok encountered by Leia; leads the Bright Tree tribe into alliance with the Rebels to bring down the shield generator protecting Death Star II.
- **Warwick Davis multi-role note**: Also played **Wollivan** (TFA cantina), **Weazel** (TPM + Solo), and **Wald** (Rodian boy on Tatooine, TPM). See `SKILL.md` for the full table.

### Cultural notes — Tusken Raiders

- Also called **Sand People** (colonial human term).
- Nomadic desert people indigenous to Tatooine. Extended kin-group tribes. Ritual combat with gaderffii sticks. Riding banthas.
- *Attack of the Clones*: Tuskens kill Shmi Skywalker → Anakin's first full dark-side act (massacre of the camp) — a major piece of the fall arc.
- *Book of Boba Fett*: Boba is taken in by a Tusken tribe after the Sarlacc; earns his gaderffii in a vision quest (S1E2). Boba's tribe is massacred by the Pykes.

### Jawas

- Small hooded scavengers on Tatooine. Speak Jawaese. Drive sandcrawlers. Traders-slash-thieves of droids.

## Canon Resolution Notes (when sources disagree)

- **Qui-Gon's Force Ghost status**: Qui-Gon first mastered the Cosmic Force technique; he taught Yoda (Clone Wars S6 "Sacrifice" arc) and Obi-Wan. Yoda and Obi-Wan then passed the technique to Anakin's redeemed spirit. Explains why Qui-Gon has no visible Force Ghost in RotS despite the implication.
- **Palpatine's clone body / Exegol**: Introduced in TRoS; elaborated in *The Rise of Skywalker: Expanded* novelization + Thrawn: Ascendancy. Also threaded through The Mandalorian via the Moff Gideon midichlorian-cloning-of-Snoke plot.
- **Grogu's rescue from the Jedi Temple**: Unrevealed as of 2026-04. Fans speculate Mace Windu, Yaddle (same species), or Order 66 survivor; canon has not confirmed.
- **Sabine Wren's Force sensitivity** (Ahsoka S1): Controversial — Rebels established her as non–Force-sensitive. Ahsoka show retcons her as very low midichlorian count, trainable with effort. Debate is ongoing.
- **Rey Skywalker's lineage**: Granddaughter of Palpatine (TRoS reveal). Takes the Skywalker name at film's end, adopted in spirit.
