# @g-marketing-geo

Generative Engine Optimization — get your product discovered inside AI tools
like ChatGPT, Claude, Perplexity, and Gemini.

## Usage
```
@g-marketing-geo              # Full GEO audit and recommendations
@g-marketing-geo --audit      # Score current GEO readiness (0-10)
@g-marketing-geo --faq        # Generate FAQ content for AI discoverability
@g-marketing-geo --schema     # Generate JSON-LD structured data markup
```

## What it does
1. Audits your product's discoverability in AI-generated answers
2. Scores GEO readiness across 7 dimensions
3. Generates specific content fixes (FAQ, comparison, definition pages)
4. Produces JSON-LD structured data templates
5. Writes to `.gald3r/reports/marketing/YYYY-MM-DD_geo.md`

## Why this matters
Traditional SEO gets you on Google. GEO gets you into AI answers.
As more users skip search engines and ask AI assistants instead,
GEO visibility is becoming as important as traditional search ranking.

## Invoke
Activate `g-agnt-marketing` and run `g-skl-marketing` in GEO mode.
